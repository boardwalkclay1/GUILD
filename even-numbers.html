Even Numbers

The world of traders loves even numbers. Whole numbers. Clean levels. 10, 20, 50, 100.

When thousands of traders are all watching the same even number, waiting for price to hit that level before they act, the market often reacts there — hard.

We love even numbers because the market loves even numbers. You will learn to mark them, respect them, and use them as key decision points in your trades.

This rule is still under construction. More examples and live reactions will be added.
