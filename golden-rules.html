The Golden Rules

These are the rules you follow inside the market. These rules remove fear. These rules create confidence. These rules produce good trades.

1. Not Guessing, Not Gambling

Never guess. Never gamble. Every trade must be based on logic, structure, and confirmation — not hope.

View Full Rule

2. Moving Averages

Moving averages show you the direction of the market. When they cross, they form the Golden Cross — one of the strongest signals in trading.

Learn More

3. Even Numbers

Traders love even numbers. The world of traders moves together — and when everyone is waiting for the same even number, the stock often reacts there.

We love even numbers because the market loves even numbers.

Learn More

4. Chart Patterns

Knowing chart patterns is one of the most valuable skills you can have. A rounding bottom, a double bottom, a double top, a flag, a trend break — these patterns tell you exactly what the stock is preparing to do.

View Patterns

5. Support & Resistance

Support and resistance are the backbone of price movement. These levels show you where the stock is likely to bounce, reverse, or break.

Mastering these levels is one of the most important skills in trading.

Learn More

6. Look Left

The history of the stock is always recorded. To know where the price is going, you must look left — back into the past to see where support and resistance were hit the most.

The answers are always to the left. Not anywhere else.
