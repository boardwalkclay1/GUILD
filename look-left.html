Look Left

The market never forgets. Every move, every bounce, every rejection — it is all recorded on the chart.

When you are trying to figure out where price might go next, you do not guess and you do not look for magic. You look left.

Look to the left and find where price has reacted before. Where did it bounce the most? Where did it reject the most? Those are your true support and resistance levels.

This is where you get your information — not from noise, not from hype, but from the actual history of the stock.
