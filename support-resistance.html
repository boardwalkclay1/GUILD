Support & Resistance

Support and resistance are the backbone of price movement. Support is where price tends to stop falling. Resistance is where price tends to stop rising.

These levels are where decisions are made — where buyers and sellers fight. Knowing these levels gives you a map of where price is likely to react.

When you combine support and resistance with the other Golden Rules, you stop feeling lost and start seeing structure. You will know where you are in the move — not just guessing in the dark.
