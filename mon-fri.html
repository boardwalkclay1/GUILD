Mon–Fri: The Weekly Decay Window

Inside the Guild, you will learn one of the most important truths about options trading: contracts lose value every single day of the week. This is called time decay, and it is the reason Friday becomes our greatest opportunity.

As the week goes on, contracts get cheaper and cheaper. By Friday, they are at their absolute lowest price — and that is when the Guild strikes.

Why Friday Is Our Million Dollar Window

On Friday, options contracts are so cheap that people buy them in massive quantities. When the market moves, it moves hard — because thousands of traders are loading up on low‑priced contracts at the same time.

This is where the Guild finds its opportunity. Contracts worth $5, $4, $3, $2, even $1 become available — and you can buy hundreds or even thousands at a time.

When the market makes its move, those cheap contracts multiply fast. This is the foundation of the Million Dollar Move.

Understanding the Pattern

• Contracts lose value every day • The closer you get to expiration, the faster they decay • Friday contracts are the cheapest • Cheap contracts = more quantity • More quantity = more multiplication • More multiplication = the Million Dollar Move

This is the rhythm of the market. This is the weekly cycle we take advantage of. This is the window where discipline meets opportunity.
