The Weekly Decay Room

Welcome to the chamber where you learn one of the most powerful truths in options trading: contracts lose value every single day of the week. This decay is not random — it is predictable, reliable, and exploitable.

As the week moves from Monday to Friday, options contracts become cheaper and cheaper. And on Friday, they reach their lowest point — the moment the Guild calls The Million Dollar Window.

Why Friday Is Our Strike Day

On Friday, contracts are so cheap that traders buy them in massive quantities. When the market moves, it moves hard — because thousands of traders are loading up on low‑priced contracts at the same time.

This is where the Guild finds its opportunity. Contracts worth $5, $4, $3, $2, even $1 become available — and you can buy hundreds or even thousands at a time.

When the market makes its move, those cheap contracts multiply fast. This is the foundation of the Million Dollar Move.

Friday Strike Checklist

Before you take a Friday trade, you must confirm these steps:

  • ✔ Contract is cheap (under $5 preferred)
  • ✔ Volume is high
  • ✔ Market direction is clear
  • ✔ Support or resistance level is nearby
  • ✔ You are emotionally calm and disciplined
  • ✔ You understand the risk of zeroing out

If all six are true, you are ready to strike.

Understanding the Pattern

• Contracts lose value every day • The closer you get to expiration, the faster they decay • Friday contracts are the cheapest • Cheap contracts = more quantity • More quantity = more multiplication • More multiplication = the Million Dollar Move

This is the rhythm of the market. This is the weekly cycle we take advantage of. This is the window where discipline meets opportunity.
