Brokers & Banking Setup

To beat the arena, you will need a banker and a broker. And you can have more than one.

If you don’t already have a bank account, this is your first step. You will connect your bank account to your broker so you can move money in and out.

This is a small step and it is . Do not get intimidated. You’ve signed up for apps before — this is the same thing, just more powerful.

🏦 Chase Bank (Bank + Broker‑Ready) ›

Chase is the Guild’s preferred funding engine. You can deposit money and often use it the same day — fast, simple, reliable.

Chase is also powerful because it pairs well with multiple brokers, and in some cases can act as both your banker and gateway to brokerage. That means fewer moving parts and more convenience.

This is a small, easy signup. Don’t overthink it — follow the prompts, create your account, and you’re done.

🏦 Bank of America ›

Bank of America is a large, stable bank with wide coverage and solid mobile tools. It connects easily to most major brokers.

If you already bank here, you’re in a good position — you just need to link it to your broker. If not, signing up is straightforward and familiar.

Remember: this is just a tool. A few minutes of setup now gives you a lifetime of funding power for your trading.

Open Bank of America Account
🏦 TD Ameritrade (Bank + Broker) ›

TD Ameritrade is powerful because it can function as both your banker and broker. That means your money, your trading, and your tools can live under one roof.

It’s extremely stable, respected, and built for serious traders. Funding may take a bit longer than instant apps, but once you’re set up, the experience is smooth and professional.

Again — this is a small step. Don’t let the “big name” intimidate you. It’s just an online form and a login.

Open TD Ameritrade Account
🏦 Wells Fargo ›

Wells Fargo is another large, widely available bank that connects easily to most brokers. If you see a branch in your city, you can use it as your funding base.

The online signup is simple. Once created, you can link it to Robinhood, Webull, or any other broker you choose.

Open Wells Fargo Account
🏦 Capital One ›

Capital One offers strong online banking with easy app‑based control. It’s a good option if you prefer a fully digital experience.

Once your account is open, you can connect it to your broker and move money in and out with just a few taps.

Open Capital One Account

You only need one bank to start — but having more than one gives you flexibility. The important thing is: do not stall here. This is a quick, simple setup.

Primary Trading Brokers

Once your bank is ready, you connect it to your broker. The broker is the arena where your orders are executed.

📈 Robinhood ›

Robinhood gives you an instant $2,000 margin loan the moment you open the account. This means you can trade immediately — even before your deposit clears.

It also offers same‑day access when you deposit with a debit card. Fast, simple, and beginner‑friendly.

Open Robinhood
📊 Webull ›

Webull gives you instant buying power when you deposit with a debit card. It mirrors Robinhood’s speed but with more advanced charting tools.

ACH deposits take a few days — but debit card deposits are instant.

Open Webull
⚙️ Tradier ›

Tradier is a professional‑grade broker used by serious options traders. It has fast fills, clean executions, and strong API support.

It is not instant like Robinhood or Webull, but it is extremely reliable.

Open Tradier
🏛️ TD Ameritrade (Broker) ›

TD Ameritrade is one of the most respected brokers in the world. It has powerful tools, fast executions, and deep options data.

Funding takes a few days, but once active, it is extremely stable — especially strong when paired with its banking side.

Open TD Ameritrade
🏛️ Fidelity ›

Fidelity is a long‑standing, trusted broker with excellent customer service. It is slower to fund, but extremely reliable for long‑term trading.

You can use Fidelity to beat the arena — it just isn’t as fast as Robinhood or Webull.

Open Fidelity

You can have more than one broker. One might give you instant buying power, another better tools, another more stability. The Guild uses multiple — you can too.
