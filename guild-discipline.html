Guild Discipline

Guild Discipline is the foundation of everything you will ever do inside the market. Before you even think about entering a trade, you must master your physical discipline, your emotional discipline, and your daily habits.

These rules are not optional. They are not suggestions. They are the lifestyle required to build real wealth.

Wake Up On Time

You cannot trade if you cannot wake up. You must be awake, alert, and focused by 9:00 AM every single day. No exceptions. No excuses.

The market rewards the prepared — not the tired.

Prepare the Night Before

Your morning should never be chaos.

The night before, you must:

• Study your stocks
• Review your watchlist
• Clean your space
• Charge your devices
• Set your alarms
• Handle your chores

When the morning comes, you should be stepping into clarity — not confusion.

No Partying, No Drinking

You cannot go out drinking, partying, or staying up late and expect to wake up and make a good trade. The market does not care about your hangover.

Discipline means sacrifice. Wealth requires focus. You cannot live like everyone else and expect results nobody else has.

Eyes on the Market

You must be watching the market every day. Not trading every day — watching every day.

The Guild is a community of disciplined traders. We move with intention. We prepare with precision. We walk the path to real wealth together.

These discipline rules are still under construction. More will be added as the Guild grows.
