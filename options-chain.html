The Options Chain

The options chain is the **control panel of your trading life**. This is the screen you will use to enter and exit every single trade inside the Guild. Once you learn how to read it, you will understand exactly where the Million Dollar Move begins.

Every contract you will ever buy — every $1 → $10 move — starts right here. This is the screen you must become familiar with, because you will use it for the rest of your life.

What the Options Chain Shows You

The chain displays every available contract for a stock:

• Strike price • Expiration date • Bid price • Ask price • Last traded price • Volume • Open interest • Implied volatility • Greeks (Delta, Theta, etc.)

But inside the Guild, we simplify everything down to the **contract price** — because that is what multiplies.
