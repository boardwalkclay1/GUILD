Moving Averages

Moving averages are one of the cleanest ways to see the direction of the market. They smooth out the noise and show you where price has been traveling over time.

When a shorter moving average crosses above a longer one, it forms what traders call the Golden Cross — a powerful sign of potential strength and continuation.

You will learn to watch how these lines move, how they cross, and how they guide your entries and exits. They are not magic — they are structure. And structure is what removes fear.
